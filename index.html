Festival News

Welcome to our 2019 summer shows!

Thank you for making last summer's event a major success! We are ready to have another great event in Brooklyn and we have some new things in store for you. In addition to the wonderful food trucks that were there last year, we have some new additions. For all of our foodies we will have Bamboo Bites and their Thai delights, Tacos el Bronco coming through with some of the best tacos and we'll be adding more as the dates near. Check back often!

Dj GG in the house!

Movies start promptly at 6 but come an hour early and enjoy the dope beats of DJ GG! She will be mixing up the music and playing a little bit of something for everyone to enjoy. We keep it kid friendly so feel free to come with the kids in tow! There will be dancing on the lawn and a good time to be had by all even before the shows start.
